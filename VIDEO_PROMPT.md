# Hero Section Video Creation Prompt

## Video Specifications for WeSellAll Marketplace Hero Section

### Technical Requirements:
- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds (will loop seamlessly)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) minimum, 4K preferred
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec) and WebM for web optimization
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 landscape
- **Frame Rate**: 24-30fps
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (under 10MB if possible)
- **Loop**: Must loop seamlessly (end frame should match start frame)

### Visual Style:
- **Mood**: Modern, energetic, trustworthy, professional
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Primary: Deep Blue (#004E89) - brand-blue
  - Accent: Vibrant Orange (#ff6933) - brand-orange
  - Background: Clean white/light tones
- **Style**: Cinematic, smooth camera movements, professional quality
- **Lighting**: Bright, well-lit, optimistic

### Content Theme:
Create a dynamic background video showcasing the essence of a Nigerian marketplace:

**Option 1: Urban Marketplace Energy**
- Smooth aerial/drone shots of bustling Nigerian cities (Lagos, Abuja)
- People buying and selling in modern marketplaces
- Technology integration (phones, laptops, digital transactions)
- Diverse products: cars, electronics, real estate, fashion
- Happy interactions between buyers and sellers
- Modern Nigerian lifestyle - urban, tech-savvy, connected

**Option 2: Product Showcase Montage**
- Smooth, elegant product reveals (cars, phones, property, fashion items)
- Clean, minimalist product shots with subtle motion
- People using products in everyday Nigerian settings
- Seamless transitions between different product categories
- Modern, professional aesthetic

**Option 3: Abstract/Geometric Motion**
- Animated geometric shapes in brand colors (blue and orange)
- Smooth flowing patterns and gradients
- Abstract marketplace symbols (handshakes, shopping bags, location pins)
- Modern, tech-forward aesthetic
- Subtle, non-distracting motion

### Camera Movement:
- Slow, smooth movements (no jarring cuts)
- Gentle pans, tilts, or slow zooms
- Aerial/drone shots work well
- Avoid fast cuts or rapid movements
- Maintain visual consistency throughout

### Key Elements to Include:
1. **Nigerian Context**: Subtle references to Nigeria (Nigerian flag colors, recognizable landmarks, Nigerian people)
2. **Technology**: Modern devices, digital interfaces, online marketplace concept
3. **Trust & Security**: Visual cues of safety, verification, secure transactions
4. **Diversity**: Show diverse people, products, and locations across Nigeria
5. **Success**: Happy customers, successful transactions, positive energy

### What to Avoid:
- ❌ Fast-paced, distracting motion
- ❌ Too much text or graphics (text will be overlaid)
- ❌ Dark or moody tones (keep it bright)
- ❌ Cluttered or busy scenes
- ❌ Faces too prominent (should be background, not focus)
- ❌ Copyrighted content or recognizable brands

### Overlay Considerations:
- Video will have a semi-transparent white overlay (60% opacity) for text readability
- Keep video bright enough to work with overlay
- Ensure contrast for text visibility
- Test that text remains readable when overlaid

### Usage Context:
- This video will be used as a **background** behind:
  - Large headline: "Buy & Sell Anything Securely in Nigeria"
  - Search bar and call-to-action buttons
  - Trust badges and feature highlights
- Video should enhance, not compete with, the foreground content

### Prompt for AI Video Generation Tools:

**For Runway/Pika/Stable Video:**
```
Create a cinematic, professional background video for a Nigerian marketplace website. 
Smooth, slow-motion aerial shots of modern Nigerian cities (Lagos, Abuja) with bustling 
marketplace activity. Show diverse people engaging in buying and selling activities, 
modern technology, and various products (cars, electronics, real estate, fashion). 
Bright, optimistic lighting with deep blue (#004E89) and vibrant orange (#ff6933) color 
accents. Professional, trustworthy aesthetic. Slow camera movements, seamless loop. 
16:9 aspect ratio, 30 seconds, cinematic quality. Background video that won't distract 
from overlaid text.
```

**For Video Editors:**
```
Create a 20-30 second seamless looping background video for a Nigerian marketplace 
landing page. Use stock footage or original shots of:
- Smooth aerial/drone footage of Nigerian urban areas
- People engaging in marketplace activities (subtle, not prominent)
- Product showcases (cars, electronics, property) with elegant motion
- Modern Nigerian lifestyle scenes
- Technology and digital commerce elements

Apply color grading to match brand colors (deep blue #004E89 and orange #ff6933 accents).
Keep it bright, professional, and modern. Ensure seamless loop (end frame matches start).
Optimize for web delivery. Video will have 60% white overlay, so keep it bright and 
high contrast.
```
